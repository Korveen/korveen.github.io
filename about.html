About me

To be a creative explorer, you must posses an innate sense of curiosity. You must push through fear, and be willing to try new things — even if the results are a complete failure. Most importantly, in the face of defeat you must keep pursuing the next adventure.

I try to take this approach and philosophy into each of my projects. Whether it's web-design, game development, or drinking tea; I bring my enthusiasm and passion for discovering new ideas with me wherever I go.
