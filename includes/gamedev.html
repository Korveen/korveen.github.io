The Last Frontier VR // 2019-2021

During 2019-2021, I developed The Last Frontier VR game for virtual reality attractions in the full development chain (from idea to implementation).

Create the concept of game scenes, plot, dialogues, player interaction. Design and building of levels, scenes and locations.

Also, my responsibilities included implementing game logic and mechanics, importing and configuring third-party plugins, as well as implementation a custom plugins and tools.

3D modeling of props and game models (blocking, refinement of shapes, UV-mapping, texturing) are performed in Blender and Substance Painter.

Performed optimization of game resources, balancing levels and opponents.
